At a Glance

At a Glance

  • 01 Year of Establishment of JATIYA KABI KAZI NAZRUL ISLAM?

    Jatiya Kabi Kazi Nazrul Islam University was established by the Government of Bangladesh on 09 May 2006

  • 02 Campus Area?

    101 acre

  • 03 Number of Departments?

    The university has 24 departments

  • 04 Degrees Offered?

    B.Sc. Eng., BURP, B.Arch. M.Sc., M.Sc. Eng., M.Phil., Ph.D.

  • 05 Number of Teachers?

    318

  • 06 PABX & Information and Technology?

    PABX with 450 Line Capacity, Campus Wide Optical Fiber Backbone Network with 301Mbps Bandwidth, E-Mail, Internet facilities

  • 07 Extra Curricular Activities?

    Central & Hall-wise extra-curricular activities run by DSW Office

  • 08Leisure/Recreation?

    Student's Play Ground, Tennis Court, Gymnasium, Debating Society, Teachers Club, Officers Club & Employees Club, Swimming pool.

  • 09 Campus Facility?

    Central Mosque-1, Hall Mosque-3, Hall Mandir-1, Medical Center, University Club & Guest House, Bank, Post Office, Cafeteria, Ponds, Departmental Store

  • 10 Water Treatment Plant?

    A water treatment plant for supplying safe water within the campus
