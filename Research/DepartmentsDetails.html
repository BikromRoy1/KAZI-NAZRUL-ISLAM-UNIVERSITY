    WELCOME MESSAGE

    MESSAGE FROM THE DEPARTMENT HEAD

    Dr. Shaheena Sultana

    Professor, Chairman, Department of CSE

    The department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology is one of the pioneering departments in research and education under the Faculty of Biological Sciences. The department has come a long way since its inception in 1957. Currently, we offer undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs in Biochemistry with a clear mission to develop transferrable knowledge and skills in the students. Our department has always been dedicated to maintain a research environment for better learning, creative thinking, innovation, and leadership. The diverse research areas that are being currently focused on here are plant molecular biology, plant biotechnology, cellular immunology and environmental toxicology, molecular cancer genetics, nutrition and health science, population genetics, non-communicable diseases, neuroscience, microbial biotechnology, clinical biochemistry and biochemistry of natural products. The scope of this subject is not limited to these fields only. Our alumni have been performing excellently in their respective fields worldwide, which once again reflects the extraordinary quality of our education system.........

    ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

    The aim of the department of Biochemistry and Molecular is to identify people with Biochemistry and Molecular and provide appropriate treatment and therapy so that they can be rehabilitated in the mainstream of the society. Moreover, the department is determined to produce skilled human resources and conduct different researches in the concerned field. The department of Biochemistry and Molecular formally started its journey in December 12, 2013 through the decision of Academic council meeting in University of Dhaka. From 2015-16 session the department has started BSS honors program. The total seat number is 40, of which 20 seats are reserved for the students of the faculty of Arts and 20 for the students of the Faculty of social Sciences. For the students who have successfully completed the honors course, the department starts a regular master’s program from 2019-20 session. Apart from regular programs, the department runs a professional masters program called PMSLP. Currently total 50 students are studying in this professional program including speech and language therapists, audiologists, special school teachers and doctors. Students from both regular and professional programs have to attend mandatory clinical practices. Along with few specialist part time faculties, the department possesses 05 full time faculty members.
